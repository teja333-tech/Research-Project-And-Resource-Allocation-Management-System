# Research Project and Resource Allocation Management System

## Overview

The Research Project and Resource Allocation Management System is a Database Management System (DBMS) project developed to simplify the management of academic research activities. It provides a centralized platform for managing research projects, students, faculty, funding, and resource allocation.

The system reduces manual record-keeping, improves data consistency, and enables efficient tracking of research progress and resource utilization.

---

## Features

- Student & Faculty Login
- Research Project Management
- Proposal Management
- Team Management
- Funding Allocation
- Resource Management
- Resource Usage Tracking
- Progress Review
- Final Outcome Management
- CRUD Operations
- Centralized MySQL Database
